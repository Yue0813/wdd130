Overview

Purpose

If you are planning a whitewater rafting trip, we recommend you browse our site.

Rafting adventures in the Hudson River are fun, unique ways for your family and friends to experience beauty and excitement. In the Spring the Hudson is Big Water Adventure. The Summer is great for families and the water is warm. In the Fall the river rises slightly and a lot of people come just to view the Fall Colors.

Rafting Adventures team consists of the most skilled, entertaining and experienced rafting guides in the industry. We maintain the finest rafts and equipment on the Hudson River. Life jackets, wetsuits, and booties are washed and disinfected after every trip to ensure wearing comfort and safety.

Your enjoyment and safety is the goal of Rafting Adventures. Sign up for a tour with the professional at Rafting Adventures and get the best whitewater rafting adventure experience possible!

Audience

Rafting Adventures' trip is appropriate for beginners and families with children 8 years and up. It doesn’t matter if you are a beginner or an expert, our staff provides one on one instruction to all levels and abilities. Rafting Adventures is also the best ideal for thrill-seekers and wilderness explorers. In addition, Rafting Adventures offers a variety of different sized rafts to meet the needs of any group. We welcome camps, scouts, schools, colleges, churches, bachelor/bachelorette parties (and more). As long as you have 15+ folks, you qualify for our group discount. Reservations are required. Please call us or book online today!
